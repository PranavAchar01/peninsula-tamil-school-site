Frequently Asked Questions

When do classes start?

Classes for the 2023-2024 academic year begin on Sunday, August 20, 2023.

What age groups do you accept?

We accept students from Kindergarten through 12th grade. Classes are divided by age and Tamil proficiency level.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes! Please contact us at info@peninsulatamilschool.org for sibling discount information.

What if my child has no Tamil background?

We welcome complete beginners! We have classes specifically designed for students with no prior Tamil knowledge.

Can I visit before enrolling?

Absolutely! Contact us to schedule a visit and observe a class session.

What materials do students need?

All textbooks and learning materials are included in the tuition fee. Students just need notebooks and writing supplies.
