Our Classes

Tamil language education for all ages and levels

Class Levels

Our curriculum is designed to provide comprehensive Tamil language education from Pre-Kindergarten through Grade 10. Each level focuses on age-appropriate learning objectives that build upon previous knowledge.

Pre-K & Kindergarten

Introduction to Tamil letters, basic vocabulary, and cultural stories through fun activities and games.

Grades 1-3

Building reading and writing skills, expanding vocabulary, and learning simple grammar structures.

Grades 4-6

Developing fluency in reading and writing, understanding grammar rules, and exploring Tamil literature.

Grades 7-10

Advanced Tamil literature, poetry, essay writing, and deeper exploration of Tamil culture and history.
