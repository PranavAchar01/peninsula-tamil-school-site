Our Classes

Comprehensive Tamil Language Education

Pre-K & Kindergarten

Introduction to Tamil alphabet, basic vocabulary, and simple phrases through songs, games, and interactive activities.

Grades 1-2

Building foundational reading and writing skills with focus on letter formation, word recognition, and simple sentence structure.

Grades 3-4

Developing fluency in reading comprehension, expanding vocabulary, and introducing basic grammar concepts.

Grades 5-6

Advanced reading of Tamil literature, essay writing, grammar mastery, and cultural studies.

Grades 7-8

Literary analysis, advanced composition, classical Tamil poetry, and contemporary literature exploration.

Grades 9-10

Critical thinking through Tamil literature, research skills, advanced writing techniques, and Tamil cultural history.
